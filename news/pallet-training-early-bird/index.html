Pallet Training at Clojure/West is $1000 until Jan 27th

The Pallet Workshop at Clojure/West was cancelled. We continue to offer on-site training. Contact us if you are interested.

Clojure/West has decided to offer Early Bird pricing until January 27th, at $1000 per seat. The price will go back to it's standard $1200 after this date.

Hugo and Toni will be teaching Pallet from the ground up for two days before the conference starts, focusing on real world examples. Check out the course description.

Don't miss this opportunity to learn from the creators of Pallet at a great price.
